Frequently Asked

A few things people often want to know before their first visit. If you have something else on your mind, we're always happy to chat in person or by email.

Send us a message →

We open at 7 AM Monday–Friday, 6:30 AM Saturday, and 7 AM Sunday. Croissants and viennoiseries typically sell out by 10–10:30 AM. Bread lasts until early afternoon. Arrive before 9 AM if you have your heart set on something specific.

Yes — we accept pre-orders for whole loaves, pastry selections of 6 or more, and all celebration cakes. Use the order form on our contact page or call before noon the day prior. Pre-orders are held until 11 AM on collection day.

We bake in a kitchen handling gluten, dairy, eggs, and nuts — we cannot guarantee any item is allergen-free. Our dark rye is dairy-free and we offer several vegan options each week. Please speak to us at the counter or contact us in advance.

Minimum 5 days for standard cakes, 10 days for tiered or elaborate designs. We arrange a brief consultation on flavours and design, then a 50% deposit confirms your booking. The balance is collected at pickup.

Yes — we supply a small number of local restaurants with daily bread. We work best with partners who value slow bread over convenience. Reach out via our contact page and we'll arrange a tasting.
